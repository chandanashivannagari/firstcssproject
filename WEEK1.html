ABOUT WALMART

Walmart has a significant global presence, with operations in numerous countries around the world. It has stores in the United States and various international markets. Its international operations are managed through a combination of wholly-owned subsidiaries and joint ventures. Walmart is known for being one of the largest employers globally. It provides a wide range of job opportunities, from entry-level positions to managerial and corporate roles.
Walmart has made significant investments in e-commerce and technology to compete in the digital age. It offers online shopping through its website and mobile app, providing customers with the convenience of purchasing products online and having them delivered to their doorstep.Walmart has been involved in various social and environmental initiatives. The company has undertaken efforts to improve sustainability, reduce waste, and support local communities through various programs and initiatives.
